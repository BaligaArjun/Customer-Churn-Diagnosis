# Customer-Churn-Diagnosis
Cleaned and prepared raw data using Excel and SQL, then built interactive Power BI dashboards to analyze churn patterns, revenue loss, and high-risk customer segments.

Page 1: Customer Churn Overview
This page gives a high-level view of customer churn and revenue risk over time. It shows overall churn trends, total revenue at risk, customer status breakdown, and average order value. The goal is to quickly understand how serious the churn problem is and how it has changed over the years.

Page 3: Retention and Financial Impact Analysis
This page connects churn directly to revenue impact. It shows revenue at risk, average order value, customer lifetime revenue, and loyal customer count. The analysis highlights that churned customers account for a large share of lifetime revenue loss. Sales channel comparison shows that some channels generate higher revenue but also carry higher churn risk. City-level analysis identifies high-risk cities where revenue exposure is the highest, helping prioritize where retention efforts would have the biggest impact.
